Enlistment Procedure

To ensure safety and security, we have a well-laid enlistment procedure:

  1. Fill out the enlistment form.
  2. You will receive a mail about your enlistment application.
  3. Based on your location, our team will arrive within 3-5 working days to check your documents and verify the details.
  4. Once verification is complete, your land will be enlisted.
If you have some other concerns, fell free to contact us!
